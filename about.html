About Ditrends Mart Solutions

Founded in Juba, South Sudan, Ditrends Mart Solutions has established itself as a premier supplier of quality products ranging from Starlink internet kits to industrial hardware and cleaning supplies.

Our mission is to provide reliable, high-quality products that empower businesses and communities across South Sudan. We understand the unique challenges of operating in this region and tailor our solutions accordingly.

With a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, we've built strong relationships with NGOs, government agencies, and private companies who rely on our expertise and reliable service.

Yossa Twaha, CEO

"Our vision is to bridge the gap between global quality standards and local accessibility, ensuring that businesses in South Sudan have access to the tools they need to thrive. We're committed to supporting the growth and development of our nation through reliable business solutions."
